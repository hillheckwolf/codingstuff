Guided Tour of Yellowstone - Bechler River

Explore remote corners of the world's first national park. See beautiful rivers, streams, hot springs, geysers, and wildflowers. Enjoy four days of backpacking surrounded by natural wonder

Itinerary Summary

From $725 per person dbl occ

  • Night before - Optional stay in Bozeman
  • Day 1 - See geothermal pools and potholes in Geyser Basin
  • Day 2 - Hike to Headwaters of Bechler River and explore "Mr. Bubbles."
  • Day 3 - Ford two rivers and pass stunning waterfalls on our way to camp.
  • Day 4 - Depart Bechler Canyon and return to Bozeman

Price Includes

Professional instruction and guiding; meals as noted in the daily itinerary; 2-person tents; group cooking gear; group first aid kit, water filter; campsite and permit fees; park entrance fees; round-trip shuttle between meeting point and trailheads.

Not Included

Transportation from your hometown to group meeting point; any hotel reservations; insurance of any kind; alcoholic beverages; soft drinks; guide gratuities and items of a personal nature.
